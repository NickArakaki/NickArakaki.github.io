About Me

I am a former healthcare professional turned software developer. While I found the work I was doing healthcare to be important, I didn't find it to be as rewarding as I thought it would be. I have always had a passion for technology, but it took me years to realize that it was something that I wanted to pursue as a career. When the pandemic hit, I took it as a sign to finally make that change.

I enrolled in an immersive 6-month coding bootcamp that focused on full stack web development where I spent 100+ hours each week learning common industy tools like JavaScript, React, Redux, Express, Python, Flask, and SQLAlchemy. Since then I have built several full-stack applications, and it has been one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. I am super excited about solving problems and learning how to use new technologies to help make the world a better place.

Some of my hobbies outside of coding include cooking for my family and friends, playing way too many video games, rooting for the L.A. Kings, and occassionally noodling on the ukulele.
